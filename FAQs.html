Frequently Asked Questions

   
   

1. How do I place an order?

       

You can easily place an order by browsing our online shop, clicking add to cart and following the checkout process.          

 
   

2. What payment methods do you acccept?

       

We only accept Gcash and cash payments only.

 
       

3. Can I customize bouquet for a special occasion?

       

Absolutely! We offer customization for special occasions as long as you notify us at least 1 week before the event.
Please note that rush orders are not allowed. Contact us, and we'll be happy to assist you!

 
       

4. How are the flowers delivered, and is there a delivery fee?

       

We provide delivery services, and fees vary based on location.
Just contact us for any inquiries!

     
   

5. Are your flowers real or artificial?

       

Our flowers are crafted from high-quality satin ribbons,
ensuring a long-lasting and beautiful alternative to fresh flowers.

 
       

6. Can I include a personalized message with my order?

       

Yes, absolutely!
While there is no available option for a personalized message on our website, you can easily contact us.
We will gladly include your message for free!

 
       

7. Do you have physical store?

       

We've recently launched our business, and as of now, we don't have physical stores.
However, we may consider opening stores in the future.

 
       

8. Do you accept rushed orders?

       

Please note that rush orders are not allowed.

 
       

9. I want to send flowers and remain anonymous, is that possible?

       

Of course! If you wish to send flowers anonymously, no problem.
Feel free to reach out to us to discuss payment options and delivery details.

 
       

10. Are there options for add-ons?

       

Yes, we offer a variety of add-ons, including chocolates, balloons, and personalized cards.
You can add these by clocking the add-ons on our website!
